Return & Exchange Policy

Thank you for shopping at AURA! We appreciate your business.

This page outlines our return and exchange policy. Please read it carefully before making a return or exchange request.

General Information

  • All sales are final unless a genuine size exchange is needed.
  • We offer store credit for returned items, not refunds.
  • Returns or exchanges are only accepted within 7 days of your order.
  • Items must be unworn, with original box and tags intact, to be eligible for exchange.

Non-Returnable/Exchangeable Items

  • Shoe Care Products
  • Face masks, socks, insoles, laces, and inner wear (hygiene reasons)
  • Magazines
  • Limited Quantity Products (e.g., Jordans, Yeezy's, Nike Dunks, collaborations)
  • Markdown Products
  • Accessories

Size Exchange

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, please follow these steps:

  1. contact us at hello@aurasneakers.com .within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. State your order number, the item you want to exchange, and the desired size.
  3. We will check availability and respond to your email with further instructions.

**Note:** If we don't have the requested size in stock, we will provide you with store credit but not a refund.

Fulfillment Errors

If you receive an incorrectly shipped, damaged, or missing item, please inform us within 24-48 hours of delivery at hello@aurafootwear.com.

We will process a refund for the fulfillment error.

Sizing, Materials, and Colors

For questions about sizing, materials, or colors of specific products, email us at hello@aurafootwear.com before purchasing.

Feel free to contact us with any questions regarding our return or exchange policy.
